About

I am an artist from Ukraine. I work in different techniques, but I mostly create digital art. I am not limited by a single style, and I enjoy experimenting with various techniques and materials to express ideas.

Art feels like magic to me. It helps me bring any idea to life and create whole new worlds.

I have been making art for over 10 years. I graduated from the art lyceum in Kyiv, and now I am studying illustration in Rotterdam.

I am open for illustration commissions and other creative projects
